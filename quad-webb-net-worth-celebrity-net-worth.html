What is Quad Webb's Net Worth?

Quad Webb is an American medical sales rep and reality television star who has a net worth of $1.5 million. Formerly known as Quad Webb-Lunceford thanks to her marriage to Dr. Gregory Lunceford, Quad became famous as a cast member of the Bravo reality TV shows Married to Medicine. The show stars six women who are in some way involved in the medical industry. Several of the women are married to doctors while some are doctors themselves. Quad Webb-Lunceford is a medical sales representative and the wife of psychiatrist Dr. Gregory Lunceford, who is 13 years her senior. The couple own two dogs, Khloe and Kar'rie and supports several charities that help canines, including the Canine Companions for Independence foundation. Inspired by her love for their two pampered pooches: Khloe and Kar'rie and her passion for fashion, Quad has launched her own puppy/dog couture clothing line, "Picture Perfect Pup." Quad attended Tennessee State University, where she obtained a Bachelor of Science degree in Biology and a minor in Chemistry, before spending more than 10 years cutting her teeth in the medical sales industry. On Married to Medicine Quad is known for being very feisty, and considers herself the black Barbie. Her husband is anxious to start having children, but Quad worries that they have only been married for a short time and she feels she is not ready to have children quite yet. Quad and Gregory divorced in 2018.
