Moonrise Festival Announces Its 2015 Lineup

Publish date: 2024-07-16

Recommended Videos

After a successful first outing last year, Moonrise Festival is returning to Baltimore this August (8-9) for its sophomore effort. Taking place once again at the Pimlico Race Course, the organizers have revealed the lineup for the fest today and it truly is impressive.

Headlining will be Bassnectar and Above & Beyond, with supporting acts coming in the form of Dillon Francis, Porter Robinson, What So Not, Zeds Dead, Thomas Jack and many more. As only phase 1 of the lineup, you can be sure that there will be many more exciting announcements to come. For now, however, things are already looking pretty promising.

Tickets for Moonrise Festival are now on sale over on the official website, with GA 2-Day passes starting at $150. Take a look at the first phase of the lineup below and share your thoughts on it in the comments section.
