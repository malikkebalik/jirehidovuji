What is Lana Parrilla's Net Worth?

Lana Parrilla is an American actress who has a net worth of $4 million. Lana Parrilla was born in Brooklyn, New York in July 1977. From 2000 to 2001 she starred as Angie Ordonez on the television series Spin City and from 2002 to 2003 she starred as Teresa Ortiz on the TV series Boomtown. Parrilla starred as Sarah Gavin on the series 24 in 2005 and as Nina Schaefer on the series Windfall in 2006.

She starred as Trina Decker in the television series Swingtown in 2008 and as Dr. Eva Zambrano on the TV series Miami Medical in 2010. Since 2011 she has starred as Regina Mills on the series Once Upon a Time. Parrilla has also appeared in episodes of the TV series Grown Ups, Spin City, JAG, The Shield, NYPD Blue, Six Feet Under, and Medium. She has won TV Guide Awards for Favorite Villain for Once Upon a Time in 2012 and 2014.
