Records and achievements

Throughout his career, Don Schumacher shattered records and achieved remarkable milestones. In 1974, he made history by setting a remarkable 6.18 second time at 241.95 mph in his iconic “Stardust” Barracuda Funny Car. With major titles such as the AHRA world championship in 1973 and the Coca-Cola Cavalcade of Stars in 1972, Schumacher’s list of accomplishments is truly impressive.
