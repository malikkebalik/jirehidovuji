Zombies 3 Actor Matt Cornett Wiki | Bio

Matt Cornett (born on October 6, 1998) is 23 years old American actor and singer who is known for his work in productions such as “Game Shakers” and “I Think My Babysitter’s an Alien.” He decided to become an actor when he was 14 and then directed his steps in the right direction. He learned from the best acting coaches, and in 2012 for the first time, he appeared on a TV show called “Nightmare U.”

In 2022, Cornett was in the headlines because on July 15; he made his landing as A-Lan alongside Kyra Tantao and Terry Hu in the Disney famous film “Zombies 3.” It is a Paul Hoen-directed movie including actors like Milo Manheim, Chandler Kinney, and Trevor Tordjman. Besides “Zombies 3,” there is one more project in which we will see him in 2022. The production we are talking about is the third season of “High School Musical: The Musical – The Series.”

When was Matt Cornett Born?

In Rogers, Arkansas, USA, Matt Cornett (age 23) took birth on October 6, 1998. He was a small kid when he watched a live theatre show and fell in love with the art. Later he told his father that his dream is to become an actor when he grows up. Matt’s father, Art Cornett, sent him to the Irene Dreayer Intensive Workshop so that he could make up his mind. When the young boy got to know acting closely, his intention to become an actor became even more confirmed.

Now he started attending acting classes daily and also started giving auditions. Within a time frame of 6 months, he appeared for multiple auditions but got rejected in all of them. Cornett was about to lose his confidence, and then his mother, Rhonda Cornett, came to the rescue. She suggested he keep trying. Finally, in 2012, Matty landed his first acting job.

How did Matt Cornett start his career?

He moved permanently to Los Angeles for his acting career. Even though Matt got a recurring role in a TV series, he attended acting classes at various institutions for a long time. He learned acting from famous artists such as Irene Dreayer, Tracy Martin, Janiece Jary, Tony Rago, Scott Sedita, John Swanbeck, Cheryl Faye, and Josh LeBar.

Along with TV, Cornett also appeared on the big screen in 2013. He played Diego in Ray Cartier’s movie “Can.” From 2013 to 2015, he appeared in different productions such as “The Dunes Club,” “Future Shock,” and “Bella and the Bulldogs.” He was also in Tom Shell’s thriller movie “Inconceivable” as Zach.

Matt’s career took a beautiful turn after he landed the role of EJ in the Disney show “High School Musical: The Musical.” Before this show, he was doing minor or non-recurring roles, but his celeb value increased manifolds after this program. Later he played prominent roles in other productions also like “High School Musical: The Musical: The Holiday Specia,” “Alex & Me,” “Zombies 3,” and “The Goldbergs.”
