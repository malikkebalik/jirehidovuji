7 Kylie Leo Lip Kit Alternatives To Shop If You Weren't Able To Buy One During The Initial Launch

Publish date: 2024-09-15

Another day, another liquid lipstick, am I right? Makeup enthusiasts can't get enough of Kylie Jenner's creations, and she's graced us with another beautiful shade. But if you missed out on Monday's launch, here are seven Kylie Leo Lip Kit alternatives to shop.

Kylie Jenner, the youngest Kardashian-Jenner sister and kingpin of Kylie Cosmetics, has launched a limited-edition Kylie Cosmetics Birthday Collection to celebrate the day she graced us all with her infinite wisdom, edgy style, and unstoppable presence in the beauty world. As part of the $195 collection, she released another matte liquid lipstick shade: Leo, a "true, deep burgundy." The name is so fitting, since Leo is Jenner's Zodiac sign and she turns 19 on August 10.

The Leo Lip Kit is the 16th shade of matte lip kits, and comes with the standard liquid lipstick and matching lip pencil (but in a gorgeous gold packaging). It costs $30, and can be purchased individually or as part of the Birthday Collection. The collection is on sale on kyliecosmetics.com through August 10, so if you have your eye on it now is the time to purchase!

So pretty, right?

I can't picture wearing any other color this fall.

Kylie Cosmetics Leo Lip Kit, $30, kyliecosmetics.com

If you can't foot the price tag, or bear wait any longer to rock this color, here are my favorite Leo alternatives.

1. Too Faced's Chocolate Cherries

Too Faced's Melted Chocolate Liquified Lipstick in Chocolate Cherries, $21, Sephora

This formulation is creamy and won't dry as matte as the Kylie version.

2. OFRA's Havana Nights

OFRA Long Lasting Liquid Lipstick , $20, Amazon

YouTube beauty vlogger KathleenLights teamed up with Ofra for this gorgeous shade.

3. Kat Von D's Exorcism

Kat Von D Everlasting Liquid Lipstick in Exorcism, $20, S ephora

A long wearing, long lasting liquid lipstick that still feels comfortable. '

4. NYX's Madrid

NYX Soft Matte Lip Creme in Madrid, $9, Amazon

Leo has cherry-red and raspberry undertones, and this soft formula is the perfect dupe.

5. Milani's Pretty Problematic

Milani Amore Matte Metallic Lip Creme, $9, milanicosmetics.com

Imagine Kylie's metallic "Reign" and Leo had a baby. This sparkly, cranberry red is gorgeous.

6. ColourPop's

Rooch

ColourPop Ultra Matte Lipstick in Rooch , $6, colourpopcosmetics.com

Ultra matte, ultra amazing.

7. Anastasia Beverly Hills' Vamp

Anastasia Beverly Hills Liquid Lipstick in Vamp, $20, Sephora

I love this warm, russet red take on Leo.
