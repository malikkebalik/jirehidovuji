What is Brianna Rollins' Net Worth?

Brianna Rollins is an American track and field athlete who has a net worth of $2 million. Brianna Rollins was born in Miami, Florida in August 1991. She specializes in the 100 meter hurdles and is tied for the record for the fourth fastest run in history. Rollins competed for Clemson University and was a three time NCAA champion, twice in the 60 meter hurdles and once in the 100 meter hurdles.

She broke the NCAA record in the 100 m hurdles before turning professional. Rollins competed at the 2016 Rio de Janeiro Olympic games and won a gold medal in the 100 m hurdles. She also won a gold medal in 100 m hurdles at the 2013 World Championships and a silver medal at the World Indoor Championships in 2016 for the 60 meter hurdles. Rollins won the Jackie Joyner-Kersee Athlete of the Year Award in 2013.
