What is Andy Hull's Net Worth?

Andy Hull is an American singer, songwriter, and musician who has a net worth of $4 million. Andy Hull was born in November 1986. He is best known for being the singer and guitarist of the indie rock band Manchester Orchestra. The band formed in 2004 and released their debut studio album I'm Like a Virgin Losing a Child in 2006. Their album Mean Everything to Nothing was released in 2009 and reached #8 on the US Alternative chart and #11 on the US Rock chart.

Manchester Orchestra's album Simple Math was released in 2011 and reached #5 on the US Alternative chart and #8 on the US Rock chart as well as #21 on the Billboard 200 chart. Their album Cope was released in 2014 and reached #4 on both the US Alterative and US Rock charts and #13 on the Billboard 200 chart. Their single "I've Got Friends" reached #8 on the US Alternative chart. Hull has also released albums with Bad Books, Right Away, Great Captain!, The Tiger Society, and East on Autry.
