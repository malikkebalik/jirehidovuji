FX Extends Sons Of Anarchy Season 4 To 14 Episodes

Publish date: 2024-07-19

Recommended Videos

For avid supporters of Sons Of Anarchy, Kurt Sutter’s SutterInk Facebook and Twitter page has been a wonderful resource for news on the show. Just the other week Sutter announced that he had shot some more footage for the finale, episode 13, but he didn’t know how he would create just one episode out of it.

Well, via SutterInk, the show creator has announced that FX has extended season 4 to 14 episodes, giving the fans an extra week of Sons Of Anarchy. Here is the post from SutterInk:

FX ADDS 14TH EPISODE OF SONS OF ANARCHY TO CLOSE OUT CURRENT SEASON

Season Finale Now Scheduled for Tuesday, December 6 at 10:00 PM ET/PT
…
Highest-Rated Season of Sons”

This is fantastic news and shows that FX and its advertisers have a lot of faith in the show. Hopefully the network will think about making this change permanent. 13 episodes a year is not enough, we need more!

Sons Of Anarchy airs Tuesdays at 10pm on FX.
