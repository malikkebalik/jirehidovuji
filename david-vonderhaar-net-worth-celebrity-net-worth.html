David Vonderhaar net worth and salary: David Vonderhaar is an American video game designer who has a net worth of $10 million. David Vonderhaar was born in Arizona in September 1972. A soldier is named Vonderhaar after him in the game Call of Duty: World at War, and in the game Call of Duty: Black Ops and Black Ops II there is a setup bot called DVonderhaar.
