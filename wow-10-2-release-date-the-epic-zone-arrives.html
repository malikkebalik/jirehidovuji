As we move into the latter half of 2023, you may be wondering when patch 10.2 will be released and what the final major update of the year will bring us in World of Warcraft: Dragonflight. Patch 10.2 for World of Warcraft is expected to go live as Season 2 winds down, providing players with a wealth of new PvP and PvE content and enhancements.

When Will World of Warcraft’s 10.2 Dragonflight Update Be Available?

WoW Dragonflight Patch 10.2 has yet to receive a firm release date, although it is slated to go live in the fourth calendar quarter of 2023. Blizzard has not yet announced a precise release date for World of Warcraft: Dragonflight patch 10.2, but the company did unveil its 2023 roadmap, which indicates that the update is scheduled to go live sometime in the latter half of that year.

We know that a new zone and raid are on the horizon, though the developer hasn’t revealed any specifics about what they’ll contain just yet. The story will develop as a result of the expansion as well, with the villains becoming clearer and the showdown getting closer. With the planned addition of new obstacles, adventuring in the open world is sure to become more engaging.

Patch 10.2 for World of Warcraft: Dragonflight includes not just new content but also new ways to earn a reputation. Probably a mix of Mythic+ dungeons from both the current and previous expansions will be added to the pool when it is refreshed.
