Age, Biography and Wiki

Dawn-Lyen Gardner is an American actress best known for her roles in the television series Queen Sugar and The Chi. She has also appeared in films such as The Butler and The Perfect Guy. Gardner was born in Los Angeles, California, and raised in the San Fernando Valley. She attended the University of California, Los Angeles, where she studied theater and film. Gardner began her acting career in the early 2000s, appearing in television shows such as The Shield, ER, and The West Wing. She made her feature film debut in the 2006 drama The Butler. In 2016, Gardner was cast in the Oprah Winfrey Network drama series Queen Sugar, playing the role of Charley Bordelon West. She has since appeared in the films The Perfect Guy and The Hate U Give, and the television series The Chi. Gardner is married to actor and director Dondre Whitfield. They have two children together.
