John Deere 455 Specs, Price, Review: John Deere 455 Garden Tractor is equipped with a Yanmar 3TNA72UJ3 diesel 3-cylinder liquid-cooled horizontal-shaft OHV, 881cc engine, which delivers 22 hp (16.4 kW) power at rated rpm 3350.

John Deere 455 Garden Tractor featured a 22HP Yanmar 881cc 3-cyl diesel engine, 4×2 2WD chassis, hydrostatic power steering, wet disc brakes, shaft-driven hydrostatic transmission, and infinite forward and reverse gears.

John Deere 455 Garden tractor attachments include a 48″ mid-mount mower deck, 54″ mid-mount mower deck, 60″ mid-mount mower deck, snowblower, blade, and front-end loader.
