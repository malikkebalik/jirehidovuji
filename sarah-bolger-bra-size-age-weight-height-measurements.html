Biography:

Sarah Bolger was born on February 28, 1991 in Dublin, Ireland. She made her acting debut with her role as Eileen Cloney in A Love Divided, followed by her role as Helena Fitzgerald in the television film A Secret Affair.

In 2003, she gained acclaim for her portrayal of Christy in the film In America, for which she earned various nominations including an Independent Spirit Award for Best Supporting Female.

She then became known among television viewers as Mary Tudor in the television series The Tudors, a role she played from 2008 to 2010. She went on to play Aurora/Sleeping Beauty in the series Once Upon a Time.
