What is Leon Gorman's Net Worth?

Leon Gorman was an American businessman who had a net worth of $860 million dollars at the time of his death in 2015. During his lifetime, he was the richest person in Main. Born in Nashua, New Hampshire, Leon Gorman was the grandson of the founder of L.L. Bean. After graduating from Bowdoin College in Maine, Leon Gorman began working at Filene's in Boston. He then spent three years in the U.S. Naval Reserve, before returning to work for L.L. Bean. He served as L.L.
