What is Rick Steiner's Net Worth?

Rick Steiner is an American professional wrestler who has a net worth of $2 million. Rick Steiner was born in Bay City, Michigan in March 1961. Rick is best known for being one half of The Steiner Brothers tag team with his brother Scott Steiner. In World Championship Wrestling he was an eight time World Tag Team Champion earning seven titles with his brother Scott and one with Kenny Kaos. Rick Steiner was also a three time World Television Champion and one time United States Heavyweight Champion in WCW.

Along with his brother Scott he also won the World Wrestling Federation World Tag Team Championship twice. Rick was an amateur wrestler for the University of Michigan and finished second at the Big Ten Championships in 1983 and set a record for fastest pin in the school's history at 15 seconds. He was introduced to professional wrestling by George "The Animal" Steele and started out in the American Wrestling Association before joining the Universal Wrestling Federation and then the National Wrestling Alliance. He was in a tag team with Sting and was a member of the Varsity Club with Mike Rotunda and Kevin Sullivan. Steiner competed for TNA Wrestling towards the end of his career and sporadically wrestles on the independent circuit.
