Typhoon Egay Update: Bagyong Egay Update Signal Today

Publish date: 2024-08-23

Typhoon Egay Update: Stay informed and vigilant as Typhoon Egay impacts the Philippines. PAGASA has issued a Yellow Warning for Zambales and Bataan due to potential flooding, while Metro Manila and Cavite are under a Thunderstorm Advisory. Monitor weather updates for your safety.

Issued at 2:00 PM, 26 July 2023

The Philippine Atmospheric, Geophysical, and Astronomical Services Administration (PAGASA) has issued a Yellow Warning for the provinces of Zambales and Bataan. The associated hazard is potential flooding in flood-prone areas due to the impact of Typhoon Egay.

Bagyong Egay Update: Light to Moderate Rains in Pampanga, Bulacan, and Tarlac

Pampanga, Bulacan, and Tarlac are currently experiencing light to moderate rains, with occasional heavy downpours expected within the next 3 hours. Residents in these areas are advised to stay vigilant and take necessary precautions.

Expect Light to Moderate Rains in Nueva Ecija

Within the next 3 hours, Nueva Ecija is expected to experience light to moderate rains. Local authorities and residents are urged to be prepared and monitor the weather updates closely.

Thunderstorm Advisory No. 13 for NCR_PRSD: Moderate to Heavy Rainshowers with Lightning and Strong Winds

Issued at 1:37 PM, 26 July 2023

Metro Manila and Cavite are currently under a Thunderstorm Advisory No. 13. Residents in these areas should be cautious as moderate to heavy rainshowers, along with lightning and strong winds, are expected within the next 2-3 hours.

Affected Areas: Batangas, Quezon, Laguna, and Rizal

The following areas are currently experiencing moderate to heavy rainshowers, along with lightning and strong winds: Batangas (Batangas City, Lobo, Taysan, Rosario, Ibaan, Laurel, Santo Tomas), Quezon (Tiaong, San Antonio, Dolores, Candelaria, Sariaya, General Nakar), Laguna (Cabuyao, Calamba, Los Banos), and Rizal (Rodriguez). These conditions may persist for the next 2 hours and may also affect nearby areas.

Monitor Weather Conditions and Watch for the Next Advisory

The public and the Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Offices in the affected regions are advised to closely monitor the weather conditions and watch out for the next advisory to be issued at 5:00 PM today.

For more information and queries, please call the following telephone numbers: 8922-1992 and 8927-1335 or visit the PAGASA website at http://bagong.pagasa.dost.gov.ph.

Stay safe and prepared during this weather disturbance brought by Typhoon Egay.
